About Heloise

Heloise's is a student lifestyle and food blog based in Malaysia, created to document university experiences through cafe visits, social gatherings, photography and creative digital projects.

This website was developed as part of an academic assignment focusing on the application of search engine optimisation (SEO) strategies and user interaction tracking through web analytics.

The blog combines personal content such as food reviews, reflective entries and visual documentation to create a structured digital platform for content publishing and audience engagement analysis.

Purpose of This Website

The primary purpose of this blog is to explore how content structure, keyword placement and internal linking influence user navigation behaviour within a student-generated website.

Data collected through analytics tools will be used to evaluate visitor engagement, interaction patterns and the effectiveness of content optimisation techniques.
